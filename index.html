Platform Capabilities

Intelligent by Design. Automated by Default.

01Deterministic Risk Classification EngineRisk Engine
▾
MRP classified as Active (≥66%), Referral (50–65%), Restricted (<50%), or Excluded. WAM calculation with alert thresholds. Task mark trend analysis detecting drops, consecutive declines, and below-average performance. Fully auditable and rule-based — no black-box machine learning.
02n8n Agentic Workflow AutomationAgentic AI
▾
Reads advisor calendars, proposes meeting slots, handles conflicts, creates calendar events, and sends confirmation emails — zero manual effort. Bulk personalised subject recommendation emails dispatched simultaneously to all enrolled members at the start of each trimester.
03Degree Planner & Subject RecommendationsAcademic Planning
▾
Year-by-year visual degree planner with CP progress, prerequisite mapping, and colour-coded subject status. Priority-scoring recommendation engine suggests the optimal 2–4 subjects for the next trimester based on degree rules, MRP standing, and full academic history.
04Groq-Powered FAQ ChatbotAI Chatbot
▾
RAG-based chatbot trained on university policy PDFs. Answers natural language queries about attendance rules, graduation requirements, and institutional procedures for all five roles — within a 3-second response SLA.
05Excuse Lifecycle ManagementWorkflow Management
▾
Members submit excuse requests with subject, date, reason, and optional file upload. Faculty approve, reject, or reassign to Advisor. Advisor can reassign to ASC for final review. Attendance records update automatically on approval. Full audit trail maintained end-to-end.
06Course Analytics & Quota ForecastingInstitutional Analytics
▾
Bottleneck course detection, pass/fail rate analysis, top graduate rankings per year, and quota forecasting by aggregating recommendation demand — giving HoD and ASC the foresight to plan proactively.

About Us

Built by QuadCore, for Institutions That Care

Based in Dubai, UAE

Transforming Academic Management Through Intelligent Automation

QuadCore built AISAA to address a fundamental gap: the delay between when a member of the institution begins to struggle and when the institution responds. The platform combines a deterministic risk engine, n8n agentic automation, and a Groq-powered chatbot — all on open-source, zero-cost infrastructure.

Developed as a capstone project at the University of Wollongong in Dubai, AISAA is designed for immediate institutional deployment with no licensing fees or proprietary dependencies.

🎯

Our Mission

Eliminate preventable academic failure by giving every institution the intelligence to act before decline becomes crisis.

🔭

Our Vision

A university ecosystem where every stakeholder operates with real-time intelligence and zero overhead.
