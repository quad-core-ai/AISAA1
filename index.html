The Challenge

Students fall through
the cracks.
Advisors are
overwhelmed.

Traditional academic advising relies on reactive, manual processes. By the time a student is flagged for support, the damage is already done — failed units, academic exclusion, and lost potential.

40%
of at-risk students go undetected until after their first academic failure, when intervention is least effective.
1 : 200+
Typical advisor-to-student ratio makes personalised monitoring humanly impossible at scale.
Fragmented
Data sits in siloed systems — LMS, attendance registers, grade books — never synthesised into a unified risk picture.
Zero automation
Escalation workflows are manual, inconsistent, and often too slow. AISAA changes all of this.

Process

From raw data
to decisive action

A four-stage intelligent pipeline that never sleeps, never misses a signal.

Stage 01 — Ingest

Unified Data Aggregation

AISAA connects to institutional data sources — LMS exports, attendance systems, grade records, and MRP/WAM APIs — consolidating every academic signal into a single, live database via Supabase.

Supabase PostgreSQLRESTful APIsCSV ImportReal-Time Sync
Stage 02 — Analyse

AI-Powered Risk Scoring

Our fine-tuned machine learning model evaluates attendance trends, grade trajectories, session engagement, and historical patterns — generating a weighted risk classification (High / Medium / Low) for every enrolled student.

Python ML ModelGPT-4o IntegrationWeighted ScoringTrend Analysis
Stage 03 — Escalate

Intelligent Workflow Automation

When thresholds are breached, AISAA automatically creates academic concern tickets, assigns them to the relevant advisor, and notifies students — all without manual intervention. Case notes and resolution timelines are tracked end-to-end.

Auto-TicketingEmail NotificationsMS Teams AlertsCase Management
Stage 04 — Intervene

Personalised AI Recommendations

GPT-4o synthesises each student's profile into contextual advisor briefs, recommended support resources, and actionable next steps — so every conversation starts with insight, not guesswork.

GPT-4o SummariesPersonalised PlansResource MatchingOutcome Tracking
