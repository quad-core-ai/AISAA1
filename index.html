How It Works

From Raw Data
to Automated Action

01

Ingest & Centralise

Academic records, grades, attendance, and enrolment data flow from the university SIS into AISAA via scheduled secure exports.

02

Classify & Detect

The deterministic Risk Evaluation Engine calculates MRP status, WAM, task mark trends, and attendance thresholds for every student.

03

Alert & Escalate

When any threshold is breached, targeted alerts reach the relevant stakeholder with the specific risk area identified immediately.

04

Automate & Resolve

n8n autonomously schedules meetings, syncs calendars, sends reminders, and dispatches bulk recommendation emails.

Platform Capabilities

Intelligent by Design.
Automated by Default.

01 — Risk Engine

Deterministic Risk Classification

MRP classified as Active, Referral, Restricted, or Excluded. WAM bands, task mark trends, attendance thresholds — all fully auditable rule-based logic.

02 — Agentic AI

n8n Workflow Automation

Reads advisor calendars, proposes meeting slots, handles conflicts, creates events, sends reminders, dispatches bulk recommendation emails — zero manual effort.

03 — Planning

Degree Planner & Recommendations

Year-by-year visual planner with CP tracking, prerequisite mapping, and a priority-scoring recommendation engine suggesting 2–4 subjects per trimester.

04 — AI Chatbot

Groq-Powered FAQ Chatbot

RAG-based chatbot trained on uploaded policy PDFs. Natural language queries answered within 3 seconds for all five user roles.

05 — Workflow

Excuse Lifecycle Management

Faculty approve/reject/reassign to Advisor. Advisor reassigns to ASC. Attendance updates automatically on approval. Full audit trail.

06 — Analytics

Course Analytics & Quota Forecast

Bottleneck detection, pass/fail rates, top graduate rankings, and quota forecasting based on aggregated recommendation demand.
